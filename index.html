As a Senior Developer at Clever First Aid, I had the privilege of working with some extremely talented people across a wide array of technologies. Our suite of hardware and software products exposed me to numerous exciting challenges.

The hardware team helped me expand my knowledge into entry-level hardware PCB design and embedded programming in C++. I really enjoyed the expansion into hardware as another point of user interaction. With this knowledge I converted our Foosball table into a smart Foosball table with goal and goal velocity detection, bump detection and a live-updating scoreboard and leaderboard.

I was tasked with designing and building our customer web portal from scratch. This included working with our designer and our Product Owner to build out designs and a roadmap. I was the technical lead on the front end architecture and leaded development for the project. We used React with TypeScript which would eventually lead architecture decisions for additional products.

Later, my role expanded to include mentoring for our graduate and junior developers. I’ve really enjoyed helping them learn and grow with frequent one-on-ones, workshops and local or remote pair programming sessions. I’ve encouraged them to give talks to the rest of the team on challenges they’ve faced and things they’ve learned.
